Understanding the time bar:
Automated away — The portion of a typical workweek that AI can handle entirely on its own, with no human involvement required. These tasks simply disappear from the employee's to-do list: data entry, routine document processing, standard code lookups, and similar repetitive work. This does not mean employees are eliminated — it means that slice of their day is freed up for other things.

AI oversight — Time the employee still spends because of the AI: reviewing its outputs, handling exceptions it could not resolve, correcting errors, and ensuring quality. AI is not infallible, and this human check is essential.

High-value reinvestment — Time genuinely freed up and available to redirect toward higher-order work: community engagement, complex judgment, relationship-building, and the activities shown in each card's Better Use of Time panel.
